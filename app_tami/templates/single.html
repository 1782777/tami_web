湖南铁道职业学院模拟仿真训练系统是针对电器控制的多项目模拟训练综合平台，他由软件和外部可编程可接线的硬件组合而成。 平台采用虚拟仿真的方式代替食物，让学员编程以后马上可以在虚拟场景中看到自己控制对应设备的运行.。 平台同时训练了学员的编程能力，走线能力等。并且还有电路图的解释。 平台包含了平板小车、电机、机械臂、大小球分拣、电梯、洗衣机、等12种常见控制对象的模拟仿真。

Hunan Railway Vocational College simulation training system is a multi project simulation training integrated platform for electrical control. It is composed of software and external programmable and wired hardware. The platform adopts the way of virtual simulation instead of food, so that students can see the operation of their control corresponding equipment in the virtual scene immediately after programming. At the same time, the platform trains the students' programming ability and routing ability. And there is an explanation of the circuit diagram. The platform includes the simulation of 12 common control objects, such as flat car, motor, mechanical arm, ball and ball sorting, elevator, washing machine, etc .
